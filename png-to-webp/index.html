Why convert PNG to WebP?

Frequently asked questions

Will PNG logos stay sharp in WebP?

At high quality settings, WebP preserves crisp edges well. For pixel-perfect UI assets, compare output visually before deploying.

Does WebP support transparency from PNG?

Yes. WebP can store an alpha channel, so transparent PNGs generally keep transparency after conversion.

How much smaller will my files get?

Savings vary by image content. Photos and complex PNGs often shrink 25–80%; simple flat graphics may see smaller gains.
