How to generate a QR code

  1. Enter any text or URL in the input field
  2. Customize colors, size, and error correction level
  3. Preview the QR code in real-time as you type
  4. Download as PNG or SVG, or copy directly to clipboard

How to scan a QR code from image

  1. Switch to the "Read" tab
  2. Upload an image containing a QR code, or use your camera
  3. The decoded text or URL is displayed instantly
  4. Copy the result or open it as a link

Frequently asked questions

Is my data sent to a server?

No. Everything happens in your browser. QR codes are generated and read locally using JavaScript libraries. No data is ever transmitted.

What formats can I download?

You can download as PNG (raster) or SVG (vector). PNG is great for sharing online, while SVG scales perfectly for print materials.

Can it read damaged QR codes?

The reader can handle partially obscured or slightly damaged QR codes. Use "High" error correction when generating to make your QR codes more resilient to damage.
