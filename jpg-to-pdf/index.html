How to convert JPG to PDF

  1. Add JPG/JPEG images (one per PDF page in batch mode)

Why convert JPG to PDF?

PDF is the standard for sharing printable documents, invoices, and photo scans. Wrapping JPGs in PDF makes them easier to email, archive, and open consistently on any device.

Frequently asked questions

Can I combine multiple JPGs into one PDF?

This tool creates one PDF per JPG. For a multi-page PDF from many images, convert each file and merge PDFs in a dedicated editor, or use batch ZIP downloads.

Will the PDF keep the original JPG resolution?

The image is embedded at its pixel dimensions, scaled to fit the page layout. Very large photos may be downscaled to the PDF page size.

Are my JPG files uploaded?

No. jsPDF runs entirely in your browser. Nothing is sent to JS-Convert servers.
