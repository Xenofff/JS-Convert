Why convert WebP to JPG?

JPG is the most compatible photo format for email, social platforms, older editors, and print workflows. Converting WebP to JPG avoids “unsupported format” errors while keeping file sizes reasonable for photos.

Frequently asked questions

Will I lose transparency when converting to JPG?

Yes. JPG does not support transparency. Transparent areas are flattened onto a solid background during export.

Is WebP or JPG better for website photos?

WebP usually wins on size for the web. JPG is better when you need maximum compatibility with CMS uploads, CDNs, or older browsers.

Are my WebP files uploaded to a server?

No. JS-Convert processes files locally in your browser — nothing is sent over the network.
