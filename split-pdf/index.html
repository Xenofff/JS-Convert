How to split a PDF online

  1. Upload your PDF file by dragging it into the dropzone
  2. Preview page thumbnails to see what you're working with
  3. Enter page ranges (e.g. "1-3, 5, 7-9") or select pages by clicking
  4. Click "Split & Download" to get your files as a ZIP archive

Is it safe?

Absolutely. Your PDF never leaves your device. All splitting happens locally in your browser using pdf-lib and PDF.js. No data is sent to any server.

Frequently asked questions

Can I extract a single page from a PDF?

Yes. Simply enter the page number (e.g. "5") in the range field. You can also click the page thumbnail to select it directly.

What format are the extracted pages saved in?

Each extracted range is saved as a separate PDF file. If you extract multiple ranges, they are bundled into a ZIP archive for easy download.

Does it preserve bookmarks and annotations?

Page content, images, and basic formatting are preserved. However, bookmarks, annotations, and some interactive elements may not transfer to the split files.
