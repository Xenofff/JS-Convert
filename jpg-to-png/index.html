Why convert JPG to PNG?

PNG is lossless, which helps when you plan further editing, overlays, or archival storage. Converting JPG to PNG stops additional JPG compression cycles and works well when a tool requires PNG input.

Frequently asked questions

Does JPG to PNG improve image quality?

Converting cannot restore detail lost to JPG compression. PNG prevents further loss on the next save, but won’t remove existing artifacts.

Will the PNG be larger than the JPG?

Usually yes. Lossless PNG stores more data than a compressed JPG, especially for photographs.

Can I add transparency after converting to PNG?

Yes. PNG supports alpha. You can remove backgrounds in an editor after export — the format won’t block transparency workflows.
