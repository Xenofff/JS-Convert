Why convert GIF to WebP?

WebP often produces smaller still images than GIF with better color depth. Use it for website thumbnails, Open Graph images, or replacing heavy GIF assets with a modern format.

Frequently asked questions

Does this create animated WebP?

No. Only the first GIF frame is exported. Animated WebP requires a dedicated encoder pipeline.

Is WebP better than GIF for websites?

For still previews, WebP is usually smaller and sharper. For memes and loops, GIF remains popular for compatibility.

Can I batch convert many GIFs?

Yes. Drop multiple files and download a ZIP of WebP outputs when converting more than one GIF.
