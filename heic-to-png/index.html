Frequently asked questions

Is PNG larger than HEIC?

Usually yes. PNG prioritizes lossless quality over storage efficiency compared to HEIC’s modern compression.

HEIC vs HEIF — what’s the difference?

HEIF is the container format; HEIC is Apple’s implementation. This tool accepts both extensions commonly used on iOS.

Can I convert Live Photos?

Live Photo bundles may not decode as expected. Export a still image from Photos first for best results.
