How to Merge PDF Files Online for Free

Published: April 28, 2025 · 3 min read

Whether you're combining invoices, assembling a report, or organizing scanned documents, merging PDF files is a common task. The best part? You can do it entirely in your browser with no software installation and no file uploads.

Why merge PDFs online?

Online PDF merge tools run in your browser, which means your files never leave your computer. Unlike desktop software, there's nothing to install. Unlike cloud services, your documents stay completely private.

Step-by-step guide

  1. Open a browser-based PDF merge tool (like JS-Convert)
  2. Drag multiple PDF files into the upload area, or click to browse
  3. Reorder the files by dragging them up or down in the list
  4. Click "Merge PDFs" to combine them into a single document
  5. Download the merged file — it's that simple

Common use cases

  • Combining multiple scanned documents into one file
  • Merging chapters of a report or thesis
  • Joining separated contract pages
  • Assembling a portfolio from individual PDFs

Is it safe?

When you use a browser-based tool like JS-Convert, all processing happens locally. Your PDF files are never uploaded to any server. Once you close the browser tab, all data is gone. This makes it ideal for confidential documents.
