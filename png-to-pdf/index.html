Why convert PNG to PDF?

PNG preserves sharp lines and transparency, but PDF is better for formal submission, printing, and long-term archiving. Converting PNG to PDF packages graphics into a universally accepted document format.

Frequently asked questions

Will transparent PNG backgrounds stay transparent in PDF?

Transparency is composited onto the PDF page background (typically white). For true transparency in PDF you need specialized authoring tools.

Is PNG or PDF better for archiving scans?

PDF is preferred for document archives because it bundles metadata, page size, and is widely supported in legal and business workflows.

Can I convert PNG to PDF without installing software?

Yes. JS-Convert runs in any modern browser — no desktop app or account required.
