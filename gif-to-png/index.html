How to convert GIF to PNG

  1. Drop animated or static GIF files
  2. Click Convert — the first frame is rendered to PNG
  3. Download PNG or a ZIP for multiple GIFs

Why convert GIF to PNG?

PNG gives you a high-quality still frame for thumbnails, social previews, documentation, or editors that don’t handle GIF well. It’s also useful when you only need one frame from an animation.

Is this converter safe?

Yes. GIFs are processed locally — nothing is uploaded.

Frequently asked questions

Will animation be preserved?

No. PNG is a single image. Only the first frame of your GIF is exported.

Can I pick a different frame?

Not in this version — we export frame 1. Use a GIF editor to select another frame, then convert that file.

Why is my PNG larger than the GIF?

PNG is lossless and may store more color data than a highly optimized GIF palette, especially for photographic frames.
