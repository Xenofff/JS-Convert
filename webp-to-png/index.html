Why convert WebP to PNG?

PNG is widely supported across platforms, editors, and older apps. WebP is great for the web, but not every tool can open it reliably. Converting to PNG improves compatibility and keeps transparency.

Is this converter safe?

Yes. JS-Convert runs entirely in your browser. Your files never leave your device and are not uploaded to any server.

Frequently asked questions

Will converting WebP to PNG reduce quality?

PNG export is lossless, so visual quality is preserved. File size may increase compared to WebP.

Does transparency survive the conversion?

Yes. Both WebP and PNG support alpha channels, so transparent backgrounds are kept when present in the source file.

Can I convert multiple WebP files at once?

Yes. Drop several files and JS-Convert will package PNG outputs into a ZIP when you convert more than one image.
